Kafka & Samza – Stream processing at scale at LinkedIn

A talk at AHL (Man Group), London, UK, 03 Apr 2014

This talk was an overview of the architecture of Kafka and Samza, given at AHL, a systematic hedge fund. AHL uses Kafka for processing tick data feeds from stock exchanges.
